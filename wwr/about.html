HISTORY

Founded in 2010, Rafting Travels began with a simple goal — to share the thrill of white-water rafting with adventurers from all walks of life. What started as a small team of river guides and a few inflatable rafts has grown into a leading rafting experience provider known for safety, excitement, and unforgettable memories.

Based along some of the region’s most scenic and powerful rivers, Rafting Travels has hosted thousands of local and international guests, offering everything from beginner-friendly floats to adrenaline-pumping Class V rapids. Our passion for nature, adventure, and customer satisfaction continues to drive us as we expand our trips, invest in top-grade equipment, and train expert guides who know every bend of the river.
